# Disclaimer

ledgr is research software for building and auditing backtests. It is not
investment advice, financial advice, trading advice, or a recommendation to
buy, sell, or hold any asset.

Backtests are historical simulations. They can help you inspect assumptions
and find mistakes, but they do not predict future performance. A strategy that
looks good in a backtest can lose money in live trading.

ledgr's snapshots, hashes, event logs, replay tools, and provenance records
are research and auditability tools. They do not make a strategy compliant,
certified, regulated, or suitable for any particular account, jurisdiction, or
use.
